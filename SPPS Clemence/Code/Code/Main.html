MISSION

By providing a supportive community and close personal attention to its students, SPPS develops confident, active learners and respectful citizens who lead transformative lives.

VALUES

Our Five Core Values are embedded in the SPPS curriculum; Integrity, Maturity, Independency, Communication and Transformation are modeled by all faculty, staff, and students and are implemented throughout all grade levels.

VISION

Inspire students to think critically, pursue their dreams and change the world.
