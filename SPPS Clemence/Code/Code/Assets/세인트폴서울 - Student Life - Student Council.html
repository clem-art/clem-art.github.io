Student Council

At St. Paul Preparatory Seoul, we believe that nurturing students to develop a clear sense of responsibility will develop global citizens who transform the world for the better. We are committed to raise curious, creative, and confident learners and leaders as they grow in an environment that allows everyone to experience meaningful challenges. With our small classrooms and emphasis on character, strong community, and leadership opportunities, SPPS is uniquely able to teach students about the emotional and social skills that are necessary for the world’s future. The students are the heart of the SPPS; we believe that they should be involved and feel a sense of ownership over their academic life experience.

Student Council Organization (STUCO) is a group of students elected by the entire student body, and its members share in the responsibility for the day-to-day conduct of the institutional affairs and lead organization and implementation most exciting and anticipated annual events.

STUCO is composed of the two groups: Student Council Officers and Class Officers.

  • Student Council Officers: At the end of each academic year, the student body elects one (1) student for each Officer—President, Vice-President, Secretary, and Treasurer—to represent them on STUCO for the next academic year.
  • Class Officers: Class officers are each grade homeroom, representing each grade group in SPPS. Homeroom Representatives are elected by each Homeroom peers in the beginning of academic year. This election is separate from the Student Council Officer election.
